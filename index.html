Our Ideas

To build a Collamine that will serve as a common platform for archiving and extracting online documents and SNS content, which will greatly reduce the development and maintenance cost for data collection and information retrieval related projects and products.

Multi-Users

Allow multiple users to upload and share.

Programs

Our programs are designed to reduce the development and maintenance cost for data collection and information retrieval related projects and products

Network Traffic

To prevent daily processes in collecting and extracting high volumes of data and duplications of data.

Cloud System

To allow users to upload the files to our cloud system and make them able to download and share to everyone.

Our Goals

As enterprises around have been using high cost infrastructure and human resources to apply into daily processes in collecting and extracting high volumes of data.

Our goals is to address the big data issues as above. Currently, we have an online peer-to-peer resource sharing system which allows documents and datas to be collected, extracted and shared among the social media analytic communities.
